THE FACULTIES AND DEGREE PROGRAMS

The Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka offers the following degree programs through its eight faculties.

Faculty of Management Studies

The Faculty of Management Studies offers B.Sc. Hons. Degree programs in Business, Financial, Marketing, Tourism and Eco-Business Management through its four departments, the Department of Business Management, the Department of Accountancy and Finance, the Department of Marketing Management and the Department of Tourism Management.

Faculty of Applied Sciences

The Faculty of Applied Sciences offers B.Sc. (Hons.) degree programs in Food Science and Technology, Environmental Sciences and Natural Resource Management, Chemical Technology, Computer Science and Technology, Applied Physics, Sport Sciences and Management, Physical Education, Information Systems through its five departments, The Department of Food Science and Technology, The Department of Natural Resources, The Department of Physical Sciences and Technology, The Department of Computing & Information Systems and The Department of Sport Sciences & Physical Education. However, there is a possibility to exit at the end of the third year (completing a general degree) for students who enroll for B.Sc. degree programs in Environmental Sciences and Natural Resources Management and Physical Sciences.

Faculty of Social Sciences and Languages

The Faculty of Social Sciences and Languages offers B.A. degree programs in Social Sciences and Languages through its five departments, the Department of Social Sciences, the Department of Languages, the Department of Economics & Statistics, the Department of English Language Teaching and the Department of Geography & Environmental Management.

Faculty of Agricultural Sciences

The Faculty of Agricultural Sciences offers B.Sc. Hons. Degree programs in Agricultural Sciences and Food Business Management through its three departments, the Department of Livestock Production, the Department of Export Agriculture and the Department of Agribusiness Management.

Faculty of Geomatics

The Faculty of Geomatics offers a B.Sc. Hons. Degree program in Surveying Sciences, through its two departments, the Department of Surveying and Geodesy and the Department of Cartography, Photogrammetry, Remote Sensing and Geographic Information Systems (GIS)

Faculty of Technology

The Faculty of Technology offers bachelor of Bio Systems Technology Honors Degree and Bachelor of Engineering Technology Honours Degree through its two departments, the department of Bio Systems Technology and the Department of Engineering Technology.

Faculty of Medicine

The Faculty of Medicine Offers the Degree of Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery. It Currently consists of three departments viz. The Department of Anatomy, The Department of Biochemistry and the Department of Physiology

Faculty of Graduate Studies

The Faculty of Graduate Studies offers M.Sc. Degree programs in Ayurvedic Hospital Management and surveying Sciences and Master of Business Administration (Specialization; Finance, marketing and Tourism)
